Quark.ai Named to 2022 List of 100 Most Promising Companies Founded and Managed by Indians in the US

We are especially pleased to start the new year by announcing that Quark.ai has been included among SiliconIndia’s 100 Most Promising Companies of 2022. This prestigious annual list is comprised of particularly innovative U.S. companies founded and managed by Indian-Americans.


As you can imagine, we are extremely proud of this honor, and gratified by SiliconIndia’s recognition of Quark.ai as the technology leader in autonomous customer support. Our multi-channel platform interprets complex customer cases and automatically provides resolutions at scale with unsurpassed accuracy and speed. The result is unrivaled efficiency and scalability in Customer Support, with lower escalations, higher Customer Satisfaction and significant cost savings.
